About Us

A way too brief history of us.

  • April 2013

    Meet Cute

    Brian and Tiffany meet at a 90s singalong. Tiffany tries to flirt with Brian, particularly after learning he blogs about breakfast food. Brian is oblivious and shy. Conversation ends abruptly.

  • September 2014

    First Date

    Brian and Tiffany go on a date. It goes really well, until Brady shows up and hangs out. Tiffany is confused -- was this actually a date?? Spoiler alert: it was.

  • Fall 2014

    Pumpkin Pie Calamity!

    Tiffany is hoping for a little alone time. Brian bakes a pumpkie pie, Tiffany's favorite, as a token of affection, and asks to bring it over. Tiffany declines. Brian shows up anyway with the pie. Tiffany fake yawns a few times. Brian takes the hint and leaves. Tiffany stores the pie for later. It is delicious.

  • 2015-2017

    Love Blossoms

    Brian and Tiffany spend a lot of time together, meeting each other's families, enjoying many culinary adventures, playing intense games of Splendor and Dominion, traveling, and seeing some amazing concerts. As they come to know one another more deeply, their friendship develops into love.

  • April 2017

    Engaged!

    Brian likes it and puts a ring on it.

  • September 16, 2017

    The Wedding

    Mawwiage. Mawwiage is what bwings us togethew today. We hope you can join us as we embark on the blessed adventure of marriage.
